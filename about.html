Guide & Features

Logging In with your Student ID

Access to CCS AI is restricted to officially registered CCS students. From the Welcome screen, tap Enter as Student, then enter your Student ID Number.

✅ If Registered: If your ID is found in the database, you'll enter the Student Hub. Your full name, major, year level, and department are loaded automatically — no need to fill up forms.

❌ NOT Registered / ID not found? You cannot enter directly. You will be redirected to the Registration Request Form. Fill it out and submit.

📬 Check Status in Inbox: After submitting your request, go to your Inbox. You will see the status of your application here: PENDING (waiting for review), APPROVED (you can now log in), or REJECTED (form details did not match records). You will also receive a notification once a decision is made.

Note: Accounts are managed by the CCS office only.

Viewing Announcements

Announcements from the CCS department appear on your dashboard as cards. Latest posts are always on top. Pull down to refresh and check for new updates.

Tap any announcement to see full details, images, and links. You will receive push notifications for new announcements even when the app is closed.

Pin Post & Remind Me

📌 Pin Post: Tap to pin important announcements. They will stay at the very top of your feed and highlighted so you won't miss them. Tap again to unpin.

⏰ Remind Me: Set a specific date and time for any post. When the time comes, your phone will notify you.

🔔 Continuous Notification: Reminders will ring LOUD and CONTINUOUSLY until you open the app or tap the "Stop" button on the notification. This works even if the app is closed or phone is locked.

AI Assistant

The AI Assistant helps answer questions about CCS personnel, faculty, offices, and department information. Look for the floating button at the bottom-right of your screen to open the chat.

You can type your questions or tap the microphone for voice input. Speak clearly — the message sends automatically after you pause, and the assistant replies with voice and text.

🤫 SECRET FEATURE: You can change the Chat Theme! Available styles: Soft Blue Bubbles, Pink Purple Dreams, Dark Smoke, Green Abstract, Minimalist Floral, and White Jasmine.
Only Mark knows about this — keep it secret!

Try asking: "Who is the dean of CCS" or "Who is Mark?"

Viewing Images

Tap any image in an announcement to view it in fullscreen. You can zoom in/out, and swipe left or right to see multiple images.

*Download feature is currently not available.

Settings & Profile

Access your Profile tab to customize your experience:

  • Random Avatar: If you haven't uploaded a photo, a unique avatar is automatically generated based on your ID. It changes design randomly every time you open or refresh the app.
  • Upload Photo: Tap your avatar to set your own profile picture.
  • Profile Info: Name, ID, Major, Year, Dept, and College are read-only — sourced directly from CCS records.
  • Dark Mode: Toggle between Off (light), On (dark), or System (automatic)
  • Accent Color: Choose your preferred app theme color

Use the Logout button at the bottom of your Profile page to sign out.
